# Assessor Guidance

Independent assessors supply objective assessment and validation services for FedRAMP. Their work helps FedRAMP, agencies, and providers understand whether required security outcomes have been demonstrated.

Assessors must maintain independence, follow applicable FedRAMP rules, and supply assessment information that is clear, complete, and usable by FedRAMP and agencies.
