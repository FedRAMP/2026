# Rev5 Overview

Rev5 assessor work is based on National Institute of Standards and Technology Special Publication 800-53 control baselines and FedRAMP assessment expectations. Assessors help determine whether provider implementations satisfy the applicable requirements and whether assessment materials support agency and FedRAMP review.

CR26 separates human-readable guidance from machine-generated Rev5 rule pages so that detailed requirements can remain aligned with FedRAMP machine-readable rules.
