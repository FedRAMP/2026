# Critical Timelines for CR26

| date | vibe |
| -- | -- |
| 2026 June 30 | All CR26 rules are optionally in effect and may be adopted unless specified otherwise. |
| 2026 July 28 | FedRAMP Ready goes legacy; no FedRAMP Ready submissions will be accepted after this date. |
| 2026 December 31 | All CR26 rules take effect and are now mandatory unless specified otherwise. |
| 2028 December 31 | All CR26 rules expire; stakeholders must have adopted CR27 or CR28 rules at this time. |
