# Provider Guidance

Cloud service providers use CR26 to understand how to prepare for FedRAMP Certification, supply required information, maintain certification, and work with agencies, assessors, advisors, and FedRAMP.

Providers remain responsible for their cloud services, the accuracy of supplied information, the completeness of evidence, and compliance with the rules that apply to their certification type, path, and class.
