# Advisor Guidance

Advisors help cloud service providers understand FedRAMP expectations, prepare materials, and navigate certification planning. Advisors can support readiness and coordination, but providers remain responsible for their cloud services and for the accuracy of information supplied to FedRAMP, agencies, and assessors.

Advisor participation should be transparent. Marketplace listing information and provider materials should make the advisor's role clear when advisor services are relevant to a provider's FedRAMP work.
