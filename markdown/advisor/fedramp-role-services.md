# FedRAMP's Role and Services

FedRAMP defines rules for advisor Marketplace participation and may publish advisor listing information. FedRAMP does not endorse an advisor's work beyond the specific listing or recognition information supplied through FedRAMP channels.

Advisors should use FedRAMP rules to understand what information must be supplied for Marketplace listing and what claims may be made about advisor services.
