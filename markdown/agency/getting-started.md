# Getting Started

Agencies should begin by identifying the cloud service, the intended federal use, the likely impact level, and the FedRAMP Certification information available for that service.

The next step is to review the Marketplace listing, package materials, certification class, certification path, and any monitoring information supplied by the provider. Agencies should then determine whether the FedRAMP information is sufficient for the agency authorization decision or whether agency-specific requirements are needed.
