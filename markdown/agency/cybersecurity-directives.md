# Cybersecurity Directives

Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency emergency directives and binding operational directives may apply to federal agency use of cloud services. Agencies remain responsible for understanding those directives and applying them to their systems and contracts when applicable.

FedRAMP Certification information can support agency implementation, but it does not replace agency obligations under government-wide cybersecurity directives.
