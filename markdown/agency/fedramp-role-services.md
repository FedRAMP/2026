# FedRAMP's Role and Services

FedRAMP supplies government-wide rules, shared documentation, Marketplace information, and coordination services that help agencies reuse cloud security information. FedRAMP does not replace agency authorization authority or agency risk acceptance.

For agencies, FedRAMP's services include maintaining certification information, supporting shared security practices, clarifying common responsibilities, and helping agencies understand how to use provider and assessor materials.
